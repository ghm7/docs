$$
\begin{array}{r}
(x-15)^2 & = & 15 \\
(x-15) (x-15) & = & x + 15 \\
\end{array}
$$

> [!NOTE]
>
> $x^2 - 15x - 15x + 225 = x + 15$ <- Propiedad distributiva

$$
\begin{array}{r}
x^2 - 15x - 15x + 225 & = & x + 15 \\
x^2 - 30x + 225 & = & x + 15 \\
x^2 - 30x + 210 & = & x \\
x^2 - 31x + 210 & = & 0
\end{array}
$$

> [!NOTE]
>
> Binomio cuadrado \
> $(a + b)^2 = a^2 + ab + ab + b^2$ \
> $a^2 + 2ab + b^2$

## Formula de Bhaskara

$$
x=\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}
$$

$$
\LARGE
\begin{array}{r}
x & = & \frac{31\pm\sqrt{31^2-4\cdot1\cdot210}}{2\cdot1} \\[10pt]
x & = & \frac{31\pm\sqrt{961-840}}{2} \\[10pt]
x & = & \frac{31\pm\sqrt{121}}{2} \\[10pt]
x & = & \frac{31+\sqrt{961-840}}{2} = \frac{42}{2} = 21 \\[10pt]
x & = & \frac{31-\sqrt{961-840}}{2} = \frac{20}{2} = 10 \\[10pt]
\end{array}
$$

> En el contexto del problema cumple 21 años.
