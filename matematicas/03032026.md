# Logaritmo

## $0.5^{10}*1.5^x > 1$

## $0.5^{10}*1.5^x = 1$

## $0.0009765*1.5^x = 1$

## $1.5^x = \frac{1}{0.0009}$

## $1.5^x = 1024$

## $\log_{1.5}(1024) = \frac{\log(1024)}{\log(1.5)} = 17.095$

> [!NOTE]
>
> ## Logaritmo vs Raíz cubica
>
> ## $2^3 = 8$ -> $\sqrt[3]{8} = 2$
>
> ## $2^3 = 8$ -> $\log_{2}(8) = 3$
>
> Logaritmo de base 2
